OUR STORY

More than 80% of global poor or around 500 million households globally are farmers or rely on farming for their daily lives. And these farmers suffer further more due to farm waste loss with unsellable harvests and lower market demand, new goverment standards and regulations, and high cost of commercial feed additives and fertilizers. We ourselves experienced these with our families that came from farming in Southeast Asia.
And we want to solve these.

OUR SOLUTION

Creating a new class of bacterial culture that has the ability to transform different food waste to post-biotic products that could be utilized for different purposes such as feed additive for poultry, bovine, seafood, pets, etc., and as fertilizer for agriculture needs

Providing these high value farm products at lower cost than commercial ones to farmers by either enabling them to make it own their own using their waste products or buying the post-biotic products directly from us while selling their farm scraps in exchange.

JOIN US IN HELPING SMALL SCALE FARMERS UTILIZE THEIR WASTE AND TRANSFORM THEM TO HIGH VALUE FARM PRODUCTS!
